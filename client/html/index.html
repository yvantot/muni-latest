Help

Make the best of Pop App

Welcome to POP-APP, your all-in-one study sidekick built right into your browser!

We're BSCS students who got tired of boring, stressful review sessions—so we made something smarter. POP-APP helps you remember anything using science-backed learning techniques like spaced repetition and active recall. It's not just another flashcard tool—it's a full-on memory system that runs while you browse, chill, or study.

Here's how it works:

Hit the + button to create a module—like “English.” Inside that, make units (Grammar, Vocabulary, Literature). Then fill each unit with cards—short facts, fun tidbits, or key questions. POP-APP keeps track of what you know and when you should see it again, so your brain never forgets.

Cards appear on your new tab or any browsing page (except your whitelisted sites!)—a gentle, in-browser nudge to keep your memory in shape. Choose between two smart scheduling algorithms—the SuperMemo-2 system for long-term mastery, or our level-based mode for quick review bursts.

And if typing cards feels like work? Just let our AI generator handle it. Feed it your notes, links, or text—it'll whip up flashcards automatically so you can focus on learning, not formatting.

POP-APP is designed for the modern student—fast, light, and always there when you open a tab. Whether you're cramming for exams, picking up a new language, or just trying to remember stuff better, we built this to make learning part of your everyday flow.

Study smart. Stay sharp. POP-APP's got your back—even when TikTok's calling.
